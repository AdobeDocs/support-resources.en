# Adobe Support & Tools Guide overview

The **Adobe Support & Tools Guide** is your central resource for navigating Adobe's support ecosystem. It brings together case management, troubleshooting practices, and tool capabilities that help you resolve issues efficiently and optimize your Adobe solutions.

## Who is this guide for

- **Administrators** responsible for support operations, configuration, and permissions. 
- **Developers** integrating or diagnosing product issues across environments.  
- **Business users** who need clear, self-service guidance to keep projects moving.

## What is in this Guide?

| **Category**               | **Description** |
|---------------------------|-----------------|
| **Support Tools Overview** | Understand the purpose and capabilities of Adobe's support tools, including how they streamline case management, improve troubleshooting, and enhance your overall support experience. |
| **Case Management**        | Learn how to submit, track, comment on, escalate, close, and reopen support tickets in Experience League, including shared-access workflows. [[1](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/commerce-knowledge-base/kb/help-center-guide/magento-help-center-user-guide)] [[2](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/learning-manager/using/faq/how-to-submit-support-ticket)] [[3](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/commerce-learn/tutorials/help-and-support/search-track-comment-escalate-support-ticket)] |
| **Troubleshooting**        | Use self-service solutions, environment insights, and recommendations surfaced alongside case creation to resolve common and complex issues. [[2](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/learning-manager/using/faq/how-to-submit-support-ticket)] |
| **Announcements**          | Stay informed on updates and service enhancements relevant to Adobe Support and Experience League. [[4](https://experienceleaguecommunities.adobe.com/t5/announcements/discover-smarter-faster-learning-with-experience-league-s-new/m-p/739750)] |

> **Note:** This overview consolidates cross‑cloud support concepts and points to product‑specific examples where helpful (e.g., Adobe Commerce Support); individual products may have additional tools or steps. [[5](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/commerce-knowledge-base/kb/overview)]

## How to use this guide

1. **Start with Case Management** to understand the support portal, entitlement, and ticket lifecycle. [[1](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/commerce-knowledge-base/kb/help-center-guide/magento-help-center-user-guide)]
2. **Apply Troubleshooting practices** during case creation to accelerate diagnosis and reduce back‑and‑forth. [[2](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/learning-manager/using/faq/how-to-submit-support-ticket)]  
3. **Reference Support Tools** when you need environment analysis, patch guidance, or operational insights (examples: tool overviews in Adobe Commerce). [[6](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/commerce-knowledge-base/kb/support-tools/overview)]  
4. **Monitor Announcements** to stay current on changes in Experience League and Support. [[4](https://experienceleaguecommunities.adobe.com/t5/announcements/discover-smarter-faster-learning-with-experience-league-s-new/m-p/739750)]
